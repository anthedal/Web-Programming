About The Band

The Cover Collective is a Boston-based cover band that has been performing together for over ten years. Each member brings a unique musical background and experience, allowing us to cover a wide range of genres and styles. Whether you're looking to dance the night away or just enjoy great music with friends, we've got you covered!
